An album that refuses to be summarized. It slips out of any genre you try to place it in. Self-Portrait in Three Colors, but the colors keep changing.

Solo is thirty-three seconds longer than it needs to be and those last thirty-three seconds are the best part. Ocean understands that mood is made in the margins.
