A love letter to a version of Los Angeles that probably only existed in the imagination of French producers who grew up on Giorgio Moroder records. Which is to say: completely sincere.

Get Lucky is the most perfect song about nothing in particular. Giorgio by Moroder is a better documentary about the history of electronic music than most documentaries.
