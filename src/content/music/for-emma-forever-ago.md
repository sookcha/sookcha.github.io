Recorded alone in a Wisconsin cabin over a winter. You can hear every wall of that cabin in the reverb. It sounds like isolation made audible without being made bearable.

Skinny Love is a breakup song that never names the breakup. That restraint is what gives it its power — the wound is implied by everything around it, never shown directly.
