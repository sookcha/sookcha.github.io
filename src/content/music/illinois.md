An album about a state that is secretly an album about grief, history, and the way faith keeps failing us at the moments we need it most.

John Wayne Gacy Jr. ends with Stevens turning the lens on himself in a moment so unexpected and honest it makes the whole record re-read itself. One of the bravest moves in folk music.
