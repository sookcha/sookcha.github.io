The most beloved cult album in indie music. Beloved because it sounds like it was made by someone who could not not make it — not a career move, not a product, just a necessity.

Jeff Mangum sings about Anne Frank and resurrection and two-headed boys with equal conviction, and somehow it is all coherent. Faith without a denomination.
