Smells Like Teen Spirit is the most overplayed song of the nineties and it is still, somehow, completely startling every time the guitar comes in after the verse.

Cobain's genius was that he hid genuine pop craft inside noise and called it contempt. The hooks are undeniable. The self-destruction was not a pose.
