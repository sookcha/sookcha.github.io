---
title: "The Problem with Ratings"
date: 2026-03-14
description: "Why I still use them anyway."
---

A number out of ten collapses everything that cannot be collapsed: the mood you were in, the moment in your life, how the book arrived, who recommended it. A 9 given in grief is not the same as a 9 given in contentment.

And yet I keep rating things. Because the alternative — saying nothing quantifiable — feels like a different kind of dishonesty. The number is a lie I have agreed to tell.
