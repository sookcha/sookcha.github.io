---
title: "Slow Media"
date: 2026-01-25
description: "Against the feed."
---

A newspaper read once a day gives you a very different picture of the world than a feed refreshed every twenty minutes. Not a worse picture — a different one, with different distortions.

The feed creates the impression that everything is happening now. The newspaper reminds you that most things happened yesterday, and yesterday is already a different country.
