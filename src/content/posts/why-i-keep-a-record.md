---
title: "Why I Keep a Record"
date: 2026-02-12
description: "Notes on notetaking."
---

I do not keep notes to remember things. I keep notes to think more carefully in the moment of writing them. The act of writing a sentence about something forces a precision that thinking alone does not require.

Whether I ever read them again is secondary. Most notes are written to the self who is writing them, not to any future reader.
