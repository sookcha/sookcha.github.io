---
title: "Attention as a Resource"
date: 2026-02-20
description: "On spending it better."
---

We talk about time management but the scarcer thing is attention. You can have a free afternoon and spend all of it in a shallow, fragmented state that produces nothing and leaves you feeling vaguely robbed.

The question is not what to do with free time but how to arrive at it in a condition capable of using it.
