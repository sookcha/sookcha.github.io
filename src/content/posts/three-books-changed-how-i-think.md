---
title: "Three Books That Changed How I Think"
date: 2026-03-21
description: "Not a listicle. An honest account."
---

The first was a history of science that made me understand how often consensus is wrong. The second was a novel that showed me how limited my imagination of other people had been. The third was a short book on decision-making I read during a bad year that I could not have read any other time.

Books land when they land. The right book at the wrong time leaves no mark.
