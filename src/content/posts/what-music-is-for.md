---
title: "What Music Is For"
date: 2026-01-15
description: "A personal theory."
---

Music does something that language cannot: it changes the emotional temperature of a room or a moment without asserting anything propositional. You cannot argue with it. You can only feel it or not.

This is why it is so useful for processing experiences that resist being spoken about directly. The album you listened to constantly during a difficult period is not a memory — it is a container.
