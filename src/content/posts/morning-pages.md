---
title: "Morning Pages"
date: 2026-03-07
description: "Six months of writing badly every morning."
---

The instruction is to write three pages by hand immediately after waking, before any other input. No editing, no rereading. Just output.

For the first month it was garbage. For the second month it was also garbage, but faster. By month four I started finding sentences in there I wanted to keep.
