---
title: "On Walking"
date: 2026-03-28
description: "Why I walk without headphones."
---

There is a particular quality of attention available only when you are walking somewhere without anything playing in your ears. The ambient sounds of a city or a trail fill in around you and the mind does something it cannot do when occupied: it wanders productively.

I started leaving headphones at home two years ago and have not missed them.
