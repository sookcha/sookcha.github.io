Le Guin uses science fiction the way it should be used — as a laboratory for testing social assumptions you cannot test any other way. Remove gender. What remains? Everything that matters.

The crossing of the ice sheet is one of the great survival narratives in literature, made devastating because we have already watched one of the two walkers become someone the other person loves.
