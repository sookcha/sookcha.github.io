Written in 1985, it predicted the ambient anxiety of now so precisely that rereading it feels less like encountering a novel than reading a diagnosis.

The supermarket scenes are the most accurate portrait of late capitalism's consolations ever put into prose. All the brand names are prayers to a god that may or may not be listening.
