The footnotes are not an affectation. They are the form made visible — a mind that cannot stop qualifying, cannot stop noticing, cannot stop caring about every parenthetical consequence.

The tennis sections and the halfway house sections never intersect plotwise and that is the point. Two Americas sharing a border, unable to see each other. The funniest sad book or the saddest funny book.
